Ramen

Ramen is a Japanese noodle dish. In english Ramen literally means, quick cooking noodles. Commonly it is served in a broth with meat and vegetables.

Ingredients

  • Noodles
  • Broth, chicken, pork, or miso usually
  • Vegetables, Bok choy, green onions, corn, or bean sprouts
  • Eggs, hardboiled in soy sauce
  • Toppings, black sesame, fish cakes, etc
  1. Prepare broth
  2. Boil noodles
  3. Add noodles to simmering broth
  4. Add your vegetables
  5. Top with eggs and black sesame along with any other toppings you may want
