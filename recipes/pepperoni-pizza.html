Pepperoni Pizza

Pepperoni Pizza is a common and world wide dish. It is usually cooked dough topped with tomato sauce, cheese, and pepperonis.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough ball
  • Tomato pizza sauce
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Pepperonis
  • Fresh oregano
  1. Roll out dough using rolling pin
  2. Add pizza sauce on top, spreading to cover whole pizza except for the very edges
  3. Cover with shredded Mozzarella cheese
  4. Place pepperonis on top of the cheese
  5. Bake for 20-25 minutes in oven at 500 degrees
  6. Take pizza out, garnish with fresh oregano
